Welcome to NeuroStudy

NeuroStudy is a study tool designed to:

Provide you with a clean, accessible, and interactive environment to manage tasks, take notes, and focus on studying efficiently.

  • Task Management: Add, remove, and prioritize tasks with color-coding.
  • Study Notes: Write notes and listen to them with the text-to-speech feature.
  • Focus Timer: Use a Pomodoro-style timer to structure study sessions.
  • Accessibility Settings: Adjust font size, contrast, and focus mode.
  • Persistent Data: Tasks and settings are saved automatically, even after reload.

How NeuroStudy Helps You

Stay Organized

Manage tasks and prioritize your work using color-coded categories for high, medium, and low priorities.

Focus Better

Use the Pomodoro timer and focus mode to minimize distractions and maintain study flow.

Retain Information

Take notes in the study section and listen to them using the text-to-speech feature to reinforce learning.

Customize Your Learning

Adjust font size, background contrast, and other accessibility settings to match your preferences.
